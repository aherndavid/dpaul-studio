The Job Market Has Changed

Five Years Ago vs. Today

Five Years AgoToday
Where people workedMostly in one officeRemote and hybrid are common
Who you competed withCandidates nearbyCandidates from anywhere
First screeningUsually a hiring managerOften software, before any person
What stood outA polished, general resumeA CV tailored to each role
Career paceLonger stretches in one roleMore frequent moves between roles

The New Competition

Remote work removed geography as a limit — a single opening can now draw applicants from anywhere.

Standing out takes more than a good resume. It takes the right one, for each role you apply to.

Writing A CV That Works Now

What Employers Look For Now

  • 01Skills and keywords that match the posting
  • 02Clear, specific achievements — even simple ones
  • 03A format that's easy to scan, by people and software
  • 04Consistency across your CV, application and profiles

Common Mistakes To Avoid

  • 01Sending the same CV to every application
  • 02Dense paragraphs instead of scannable bullets
  • 03Missing the exact keywords used in the job posting
  • 04Formatting that confuses screening software
